About Me

I graduated this summer from Brunel University and now hold a First Class Honours in Computer Science. I recently completed a training with Sparta Global where I have learned to work as a consultant and to think with a technical mindset.

I am an individual who likes to take on new challenges and relishes the problem solving aspect that comes along when building new applications. Software development for me is not about creating new applications, its more about embracing the constant changes that happen around technology and using those new innovations to build services that aim at helping the end user in one aspect or another.

The language which I am most comfortable with is C# however I am always open to new opportunities and would use any chance provided to me to improve my skills further.
